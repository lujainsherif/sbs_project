Who We Are?

SBS is a student activity launched in 2022, bringing together students from all faculties to foster innovation and collaboration.

What we do?

Our mission is to prepare ambitious minds for real-world challenges by staying ahead of market trends and leveraging modern technology. We focus on in-demand skills, ensuring students gain the tools and knowledge for success.

Programs

Marketing

The Marketing Committee crafting creative campaigns, managing social media, and building strong connections with the target audience to enhance engagement and visibility.

Graphic Design

The Graphic Design Committee creates impactful visuals, designs, and branding materials that bring vision to life and engage the audience creatively.

Programming

The Programming Committee develops and maintains digital solutions, ensuring seamless functionality and innovative tools to support goals.

Human Resources

The Human Resources Committee focuses on recruiting, training, and supporting team members, fostering a productive and collaborative environment.

Operation and Logistics

The Operations and Logistics Committee ensures the smooth execution of events and activities by managing planning, coordination, and resource allocation effectively.
